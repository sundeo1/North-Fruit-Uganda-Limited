What we offer


Habanero hot pepper

Availability (All year round)

We have the ability to provide 3 tonnes of hot pepper per month all year. Packed in 4 kg boxes.

Avocado

We have the ability to provide 20 tonnes of avocado per week during the harvesting season. Packed in 10kg boxes

Pineapples

We supply sweet cayenne pineapples that are available all year round. We can supply any tonnage that can be agreed upon by us and our buyers



MATOOKE(green bananas)

We also supply matooke (green bananas).we supply the kibuuzi variety and mpologoma variety. We can supply an agreed tonnage after consultation with prospective buyers. They are packed in 10kg boxes

Passion Fruits

This are also available all year round and we supply the kasese variety (purple passion fruits) that are good for export. we are able to provide any tonnage as agreed between us and the buyers. Usually packed in 7kg boxes

Garden Eggs

We supply these too in the green and white variety. We have the ability to supply unlimited quantity throughout the year. Usually packed in 10kg boxes

Quality Controller

Our quality controller at the pack house ensures the following

  • Health safety regulations are followed
  • Proper dress attire in pack house
  • Ensure that banned substances are not used and checked for in produce
  • Proper sorting and quality produce is sent for export
  • Retention sampling
  • Identifying pests like false coding moth
