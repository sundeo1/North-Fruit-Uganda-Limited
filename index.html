North Fruits Uganda Limited is an export company involved in the export of fresh foods and vegetables from Uganda. It’s a privately owned company and registered under the company law in Uganda.

North Fruits Uganda Limited was founded in Uganda in 2014 after seeing the need to provide good, affordable, safe food to clients all over the world.

Quality Control


We have an agronomist who ensures that proper practices at our farmer’s farms are followed for example

  • Proper farming records are kept
  • Tracebilty of produce from farmers to pack house
  • Proper post harvesting methods are followed
  • Proper coding when planting commences
  • Inspection records for pests and diseases

Quality Controller

Our quality controller at the pack house ensures the following

  • Health safety regulations are followed
  • Proper dress attire in pack house
  • Ensure that banned substances are not used and checked for in produce
  • Proper sorting and quality produce is sent for export
  • Retention sampling
  • Identifying pests like false coding moth
